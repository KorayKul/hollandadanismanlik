Logistical Infrastructure Advantages of the Netherlands

The Netherlands has one of the best infrastructures in Europe. For this reason, many global companies choose to locate their logistics and distribution centers here.

✈️ Air Transport

Schiphol Airport is one of Europe’s busiest cargo hubs, home to over 500 companies. With five airports across the country, fresh goods, electronics, and time-sensitive products are efficiently transported throughout Europe.

🚢 Sea Transport

The Port of Rotterdam is the largest in Europe and one of the top 10 globally, handling over 580 million tons of cargo annually. Companies near the port benefit from strong global competitiveness.

Additionally, the Netherlands provides inland waterway transport to Germany, France, and Switzerland via the Maas and Rhine rivers. 40% of all inland EU container shipping passes through the Netherlands.

🚛 Land Transport

The Netherlands is only a few hours away from major markets like Germany, Belgium, and France. With 139,000 km of roads and border-free transport within the EU, delivery is fast and efficient.

🏢 Distribution Hubs

Dutch cities are ideal for fast European distribution. The areas around Rotterdam and Amsterdam stand out as strategic logistics hubs.

No matter your industry, the Netherlands’ logistics infrastructure gives you a global competitive edge.
